How K-Means Works

Click on the graph to add points to it. Cluster the points in any manner. The goal here is to color the points such that there are “k” clusters, which is a parameter that is chosen. Run the algorithm and for each iteration watch how first the points are colored based on which cluster (indicated by the stars) it’s closest to and then the clusters are moved to the center of all of the points of its color.


This process is repeated until the algorithm converges, or the clusters stop moving. Depending on the random initialization of the clusters, the final solution may not be the most optimal one, in which case feel free to reinitialize the clusters and rerun the algorithm.
